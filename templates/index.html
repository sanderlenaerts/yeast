What is Yeast! ?

How to start

To start tracking your beer intake you will have to create an account. You can check all the beers and their data without creating an account. However, to make full use of Yeast! we advice to create an account.

The rest is simple. On the beers-page, search for the beer you're looking for using the menu on the left. Discover the characteristics about the beer. Try the beer. Add the beer to your list.
